Our Approach

Clear design, honest features, no fuss.

We built PayTogether to make tracking balances feel easy, fair, and drama-free. From trips to takeout, we keep the math tidy so your group can focus on the good stuff.

  • Sign In

    Sign up with your email, or log in with your Google account

  • Your Groups

    Create or join a group in seconds; keep trips, roommates, and projects separate

  • Add & Split

    Log expenses with equal, custom, or percentage shares

  • Favorites

    Save common expenses as favorites for instant one-click entry

  • Dynamic Templates

    Use variables like [Month] in favorites to create smart, updating templates

  • Balance Summary

    See clear totals and who's up or down at a glance

  • Transaction Details

    Review who paid what and how expenses were split

  • Settle Up

    Get smart suggestions for settling balances in the fewest steps

  • Search & Filter

    Find specific transactions with powerful search and filtering tools

  • Upload Receipts

    Generate transactions from a receipt photo so nothing gets lost in the shuffle.

  • Review Receipts

    Open any uploaded receipt right from the group to confirm the details.

  • Collaborative Splits

    Assign receipt items together so everyone pays exactly what they owe.

  • Spending Analytics

    Get insights into your spending patterns and track expenses over time

  • Personalize

    Choose from multiple themes to match your style
