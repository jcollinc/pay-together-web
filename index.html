Questions? Answers.

The quick facts people ask about most before they start splitting with PayTogether.

  • How does PayTogether work?

    Create or join a group, add shared expenses in seconds, and PayTogether keeps balances accurate for everyone. Favorite common expenses to avoid having to re-enter the same details repeatedly. When you're ready to settle up, PayTogether will suggest the most efficient payments for everyone in the group to settle their balance.

  • Is my data secure?

    All communication with PayTogether is encrypted with HTTPS and guarded by strict access controls. Only the people you invite into a group can see its balances, and we never hand that data to outside parties.

  • Do all group members need accounts?

    Only people who want to view and interact with the group need accounts. If one person wants to track expenses for everyone, they can add anyone to splits without requiring anyone else to sign up. This works great when one person manages the group while others just settle up in person.

  • Who needs PayTogether Plus?

    PayTogether Plus is perfect for power users who want enhanced personal features. Plus subscribers get unlimited favorites, detailed analytics, CSV exports, and advanced search and filter capabilities.

  • Does PayTogether support different currencies?

    We're launching with a limited number of supported currencies, with plans to add more. We will also be adding automatic conversions so that you can track multiple currencies within the same group. Until then, each group uses a single currency for all of its transactions, and updating that currency won't alter expense amounts.

  • What happens to my extra favorites if I leave Plus?

    Your five most recently used favorites will remain active. Any extras are safely stored and can be reactivated if you are within the limit. If you upgrade to Plus again, all your favorites will be reactivated automatically.

  • Can I export my transactions?

    Plus members can export group data to CSV, making it simple to run reports, share with other members, or archive history.

  • How do I get support?

    Drop a note to support@paytogether.io and we will get back to you as soon as we can.

  • Do you take feature requests and feedback?

    Absolutely! We love hearing how we can make PayTogether work better for your specific needs. Whether it's a small improvement or a big new feature idea, drop us a line at feedback@paytogether.io.
